Strawberry Banana Smoothi

Ingredients :-
  • 1 cup Fresh Strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 1 large Banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup Milk (Doodh) (you can use almond milk or any milk of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 1 tbsp Honey (Shahad)
  • 4-6 Ice Cubes (Barf ke Tukde)
    • Recipe :-
    Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Wash, hull, and chop the fresh strawberries.
    2. Peel and slice the banana.
    Step 2: Blend the Smoothie
    1. In a blender, add the chopped strawberries, sliced banana, milk, yogurt, and honey (if using).
    2. Add ice cubes to the blender.
    Step 3: Blend Until Smooth
    1. Blend on high speed until all ingredients are well combined and the smoothie is creamy and smooth.
    2. Taste and adjust the sweetness by adding more honey if needed. Blend again if necessary.
    Step 4: Serve
    1. Pour the smoothie into glasses.
    2. Serve immediately, garnished with a slice of strawberry or banana on the rim if desired.
    Enjoy your delicious and refreshing Strawberry Banana Smoothie!
