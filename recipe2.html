Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 1 kg Chicken (chicken thighs or breasts, cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • Salt to taste
For Gravy
  • 3 large Tomatoes, pureed (Tamatar)
  • 2 large Onions, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 cup Fresh Cream (Malai)
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 2-3 tbsp Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish (Hara Dhaniya)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt.
  2. Coat chicken in the marinade, refrigerate for 1-2 hours or overnight.
Step 2: Cook the Chicken
  1. Heat oil in a pan, add marinated chicken and cook until it's cooked through and slightly charred. Set aside.
Step 3: Prepare the Gravy
  1. In the same pan, heat butter. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add the cooked chicken pieces to the gravy and mix well.
  5. Add fresh cream and kasuri methi. Simmer for 10-15 minutes on low heat.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
Enjoy your delicious Butter Chicken!
