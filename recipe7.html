Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 1 kg Chicken (whole or leg pieces)
  • 1 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice (Nimbu ka Ras)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder (Kali Mirch)
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil (Sarson ka Tel)
  • Salt to taste
  • Red Food Color (optional)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Chicken
  1. Clean and pat dry the chicken. Make deep cuts on the chicken pieces to allow the marinade to penetrate.
Step 2: First Marination
  1. In a bowl, mix lemon juice, 1 tsp red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Rub this mixture onto the chicken pieces, making sure to get it into the cuts.
  3. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
Step 3: Second Marination
  1. In another bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, remaining red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder, mustard oil, and food color (if using).
  2. Coat the chicken pieces with this mixture thoroughly.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
Step 4: Cook the Chicken
  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil or on a grill rack.
  3. Bake for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred. Alternatively, you can grill the chicken over a barbecue or tandoor.
Step 5: Serve
  1. Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with lemon wedges and freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with mint chutney, onion rings, and naan or roti.
Enjoy your delicious Tandoori Chicken!
