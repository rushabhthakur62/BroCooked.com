Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients :-
For Pulao
  • 1.5 cups Basmati Rice
  • 2.5 cups Water
  • 1 large Onion, thinly sliced (Pyaz)
  • 1 large Tomato, chopped (Tamatar)
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (Carrot, Peas, Beans, Potato), chopped
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Ghee (optional)
  • Salt to taste
For Tempering
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 2 Bay Leaves (Tej Patta)
  • 4-5 Cloves (Laung)
  • 2-3 Green Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini)
For Garnish
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (Hara Dhaniya)
  • Lemon Wedges (Nimbu)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Rice
  1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
Step 2: Tempering
  1. Heat oil (and ghee, if using) in a large pan or pressure cooker over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon stick. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Sauté Vegetables
  1. Add thinly sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
Step 4: Add Spices and Vegetables
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
  2. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
Step 5: Cook the Rice
  1. Add the soaked and drained basmati rice. Mix gently to coat the rice with the spices.
  2. Add 2.5 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed (about 15-20 minutes). If using a pressure cooker, cook for 2 whistles and then let the pressure release naturally.
Step 6: Finish and Serve
  1. Add garam masala and gently fluff the rice with a fork.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and serve with lemon wedges.
Enjoy your delicious Vegetable Pulao!
