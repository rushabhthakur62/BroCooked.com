Chicken 65

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 500 g Boneless Chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder (Kali Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice (Nimbu ka Ras)
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Corn Flour (Makai ka Atta)
  • Salt to taste
For Tempering
  • 2-3 tbsp Oil
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, finely chopped (Adrak)
  • 1 tbsp Garlic, finely chopped (Lehsun)
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves (Kadi Patta)
For Garnish
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (Hara Dhaniya)
  • Lemon Wedges
Recipe :-
Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder, lemon juice, egg, corn flour, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and coat them well with the marinade.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
Step 2: Fry the Chicken
  1. Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai over medium heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken pieces in batches.
  3. Fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels
Step 3: Tempering
  1. In another pan, heat 2-3 tbsp oil over medium heat.
  2. Add slit green chilies, chopped ginger, chopped garlic, and curry leaves. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until aromatic
  3. Add the fried chicken pieces to the tempering and toss well to coat evenly.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
  1. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with lemon wedges
Enjoy your delicious Chicken 65!
