Fish Curry

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 500 g Fish (preferably firm fish like kingfish, cut into pieces)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • Salt to taste
For Gravy
  • 2 large Onions, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 3 large Tomatoes, pureed (Tamatar)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk (Nariyal ka Doodh)
  • 2 tbsp Tamarind Pulp (Imli ka Ras)
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish (Hara Dhaniya)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Marinate the Fish
  1. In a bowl, mix turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Coat the fish pieces with this mixture. Set aside for 15-20 minutes.
Step 2: Prepare the Gravy
  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates from the masala.
Step 3: Cook the Fish
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add coconut milk and tamarind pulp. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Carefully add the marinated fish pieces to the gravy.
  4. Cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes until the fish is cooked through and the flavors meld together.
Step 4: Garnish and Serve
  1. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
Enjoy your delicious Fish Curry!
