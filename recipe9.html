Keema Matar

Ingredients :-
For Keema
  • 500 g Minced Meat (Keema)
  • 1 cup Green Peas (Mutter)
  • 2 large Onions, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 3 large Tomatoes, finely chopped (Tamatar)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder (Kali Mirch)
  • 1 cup Water
  • 4 tbsp Oil
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish (Hara Dhaniya)
  • Salt to taste
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Base
  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Saut√© until the raw smell disappears.
Step 2: Add Spices and Tomatoes
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
Step 3: Cook the Keema
  1. Add the minced meat (keema) and cook on medium heat until the meat is browned.
  2. Add water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until the meat is cooked through.
Step 4: Add Green Peas
  1. Add green peas (mutter) to the keema. Mix well.
  2. Cook uncovered for another 10 minutes until the peas are tender and the flavors meld together.
Step 5: Finish and Serve
  1. Sprinkle garam masala and black pepper powder. Mix well.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your delicious Keema Mutter!
