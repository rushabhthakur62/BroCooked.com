Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 1 kg Chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice (Nimbu Ka Ras)
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil (Sarson Ka Tel)
  • Salt to taste
    • Recipe :-
    Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
    1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, lemon juice, mustard oil, and salt.
    2. Add the chicken cubes and coat them well with the marinade.
    3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight.
    Step 2: Prepare the Skewers
    1. Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes to prevent them from burning.
    2. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto the skewers
    Step 3: Cook the Chicken
    1. Preheat the grill or oven to 200°C (400°F).
    2. Place the skewers on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil or directly on the grill.
    3. Cook for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred at the edges.
    Step 4: Serve
    1. Remove the chicken tikka from the skewers and transfer to a serving plate.
    2. Garnish with lemon wedges and fresh coriander leaves.
    3. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.
    Enjoy your delicious Chicken Tikka!
