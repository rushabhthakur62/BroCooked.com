Aloo Gobi

Ingredients :-
For Aloo Gobi
  • 2 large Potatoes, peeled and diced (Aloo)
  • 1 medium Cauliflower, cut into florets (Gobi)
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds (Rai)
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 2 large Tomatoes, chopped (Tamatar)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish (Hara Dhaniya)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables
  1. Wash and cut the potatoes into small cubes.Wash and cut the potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Wash and cut the cauliflower into small florets.
  3. Blanch the cauliflower in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, then drain and set asid
Step 2: Tempering
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or kadai over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
Step 3: Add Spices and Tomatoes
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates from the masala.
Step 4: Cook the Vegetables
  1. Add the diced potatoes and mix well with the masala.
  2. Cover and cook on low heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the blanched cauliflower florets and salt. Mix well.
  4. Cover and cook for another 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender and cooked through.
Step 5: Finish and Serve
  1. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with roti, naan, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your delicious Aloo Gobi!
