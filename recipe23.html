Gobi Manchurian

Ingredients :-
For Gobi (Cauliflower)
  • 1 medium Cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 3/4 cup All-Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch (Cornflour)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying
For Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic, finely chopped (Lehsun)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, finely chopped (Adrak)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 1 large Capsicum, finely chopped (Shimla Mirch)
  • 2-3 Spring Onions, chopped (Hara Pyaz)
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Sauce
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (Cornflour slurry)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Black Pepper Powder
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Cauliflower
  1. Blanch the cauliflower florets in boiling water for 3-4 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Dip each cauliflower floret into the batter, ensuring it is coated well. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels. Set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Sauce
  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat
  2. Add chopped garlic, ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  4. Add finely chopped capsicum and spring onions. Sauté for another 2-3 minutes.
Step 3: Add Sauces and Spices
  1. Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, red chili sauce, and vinegar. Mix well.
  2. Add the cornstarch slurry to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring continuously, until the sauce thickens.
  3. Season with salt and black pepper powder.
Step 4: Combine Cauliflower and Sauce
  1. Add the fried cauliflower florets to the sauce. Toss well to ensure the florets are evenly coated with the sauce.
  2. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 5: Finish and Serve
  1. Garnish with chopped spring onion greens.
  2. Serve hot as an appetizer or with fried rice or noodles.
Enjoy your delicious Gobi Manchurian!
