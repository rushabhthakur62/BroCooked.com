Chicken Biryani

Ingredients :-
For Marination
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • Salt to taste
For Rice
  • 3 cups Basmati Rice
  • 4-5 Cloves
  • 2-3 Bay Leaves
  • 1-2 Black Cardamom
  • 2-3 Green Cardamom
  • 1 Cinnamon Stick
  • Salt to taste
For Biryani
  • 3 large Onions, thinly sliced
  • 3-4 Green Chilies, slit
  • 1 cup Mint Leaves
  • 1 cup Coriander Leaves
  • 1 cup Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp Saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
  • 1/2 cup Ghee or Oil
  • 2-3 tbsp Biryani Masala
Recipe :-
Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
  1. Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt.
  2. Coat chicken in marinade, refrigerate 1-2 hours or overnight
Step 2: Prepare the Rice
  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil water with cloves, bay leaves, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Cook rice until 70-80% done. Drain and set aside.
Step 3: Fry the Onions
  1. Heat ghee/oil, fry onions until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
Step 4: Cook the Chicken
  1. In the same pan, cook marinated chicken until partially done.
  2. Add tomatoes and green chilies, cook until chicken is done.
  3. Add half mint and coriander leaves, mix well.
Step 5: Layer the Biryani
  1. Layer partially cooked rice in a pot.
  2. Add a layer of chicken, sprinkle fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  3. Repeat layers, ending with rice on top.
  4. Drizzle saffron milk (if using) and sprinkle biryani masala and remaining fried onions.
Step 6: Dum Cooking
  1. Cover pot tightly, cook on low heat on a tawa for 25-30 minutes or in a 180Â°C oven for 25-30 minutes.
Step 7: Serve
  1. Let rest for 10 minutes, then fluff rice gently.
  2. Serve hot with raita, salad, and papad.
Enjoy your delicious Chicken Biryani!
