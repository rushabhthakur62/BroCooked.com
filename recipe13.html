Chana Masala

Ingredients :-
For Chana
  • 2 cups Chickpeas (Chana), soaked overnight and boiled
  • 2 large Onions, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 3 large Tomatoes, finely chopped (Tamatar)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Dry Mango Powder (Amchur)
  • 1 tsp Chana Masala Powder (optional) -
  • 3 tbsp Oil
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves for garnish (Hara Dhaniya)
  • Salt to taste
For Tempering
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 1 Bay Leaf (Tej Patta)
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini)
  • 3-4 Cloves (Laung)
  • 2-3 Green Cardamom (Elaichi)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Chickpeas
  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight in water.
  2. Drain and pressure cook with fresh water and a little salt until soft (about 15-20 minutes).
Step 2: Tempering
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or kadai over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom. Let them splutter.
Step 3: Prepare the Masala Base
  1. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates from the masala.
Step 4: Add Spices
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 5: Cook the Chickpeas
  1. Add the boiled chickpeas to the pan. Mix well with the masala.
  2. Add some water to achieve the desired consistency. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
Step 6: Finish and Serve
  1. Add garam masala, dry mango powder, and chana masala powder (if using). Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with bhatura, puri, roti, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your delicious Chana Masala!
