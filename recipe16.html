Dal Makhani

Ingredients :-
For Dal
  • 1 cup Whole Black Lentils (Urad Dal)
  • 1/4 cup Red Kidney Beans (Rajma)
  • 4 cups Water

For Gravy
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 2 large Tomatoes, pureed (Tamatar)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)/li>
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream (Malai)
  • Salt to taste
For Garnish
  • Fresh Cream (Malai)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (Hara Dhaniya)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Dal
  1. Wash and soak the whole black lentils and red kidney beans overnight in plenty of water.
  2. Drain and rinse the soaked dals. Transfer to a pressure cooker with 4 cups of water.
  3. Pressure cook for 15-20 minutes or until the dals are soft and cooked through.
Step 2: Prepare the Gravy
  1. Heat 3 tbsp butter and 2 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
Step 3: Add Spices
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Cook the Dal
  1. Add the cooked dal along with its water to the pan. Mix well.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer on low heat for 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 5: Finish with Cream
  1. Add fresh cream and garam masala. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  2. Adjust salt to taste.
Step 6: Garnish and Serve
  1. Garnish with a swirl of fresh cream and freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  2. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice
Enjoy your delicious Dal Makhani!
