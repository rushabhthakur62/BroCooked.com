Mango Lassi

Ingredients :-
  • 2 large ripe Mangoes, peeled and chopped (Aam)
  • 2 cups Yogurt (Dahi)
  • 1/2 cup Milk (Doodh)
  • 2-3 tbsp Sugar (Cheeni)
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder (Elaichi)
  • 4-6 Ice Cubes (Barf ke Tukde)
  • Saffron strands (Kesar) for garnish
  • Chopped nuts (Almonds and Pistachios) for garnish
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Mangoes
  1. Wash, peel, and chop the ripe mangoes. Discard the seeds.
Step 2: Blend the Lassi
  1. In a blender, add the chopped mangoes, yogurt, milk, sugar, and cardamom powder.
  2. Add ice cubes to the blender.
Step 3: Blend Until Smooth
  1. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  2. Taste and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar if needed. Blend again if necessary.
Step 4: Serve
  1. Pour the mango lassi into glasses.
  2. Garnish with saffron strands and chopped nuts if desired.
Enjoy your delicious and refreshing Mango Lassi!
