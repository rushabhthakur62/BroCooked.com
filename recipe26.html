Dosa with Potato Filling

Ingredients :-
For Dosa Batter
  • 1 cup Rice (Chawal)
  • 1/2 cup Urad Dal (Black Gram Lentils)
  • 1/4 tsp Fenugreek Seeds (Methi)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
For Potato Filling
  • 3 large Potatoes, boiled and mashed (Aloo)
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 2 Green Chilies, finely chopped (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, grated (Adrak)
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves (Kadi Patta)
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds (Rai)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves, chopped (Hara Dhaniya)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice (Nimbu)
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Dosa Batter
  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and grind the soaked mixture into a smooth batter, adding water as needed to achieve a thick consistency.
  3. Transfer the batter to a large bowl, cover, and let it ferment in a warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight.
  4. After fermentation, add salt to taste and mix well. Adjust the consistency with water if needed.
Step 2: Prepare the Potato Filling
  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, chopped green chilies, and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  6. Add the boiled and mashed potatoes. Mix thoroughly.
  7. Add salt to taste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add chopped coriander leaves and lemon juice. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  9. Remove from heat and set aside.
Step 3: Cook the Dosa
  1. Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter onto the tawa and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  3. Drizzle a few drops of oil around the edges and cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy.
  4. Place a portion of the prepared potato filling in the center of the dosa.
  5. Fold the dosa over the filling to form a semi-circle or roll it up.
Step 4: Finish and Serve
  1. Serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or any chutney of your choice.
Enjoy your delicious Dosa with Potato Filling!
