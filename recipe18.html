Bhindi Masala

Ingredients :-
For Bhindi
  • 500 g Okra (Bhindi), washed, dried, and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 3 tbsp Oil
For Masala
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • 1 large Onion, finely chopped (Pyaz)
  • 2 large Tomatoes, finely chopped (Tamatar)
  • 2-3 Green Chilies, slit (Hari Mirch)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste (Adrak-Lehsun ka Paste)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder (Lal Mirch)
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder (Dhaniya Powder)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder (Jeera Powder)
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Dry Mango Powder (Amchur)
  • Salt to taste
For Garnish
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (Hara Dhaniya), chopped
Recipe :-
Step 1: Prepare the Bhindi
  1. Heat 3 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium heat
  2. Add the chopped bhindi and sauté until it becomes tender and slightly crispy. Remove and set aside.
Step 2: Prepare the Masala
  1. In the same pan, heat 2 tbsp oil.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and slit green chilies. Sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil separates from the masala.
Step 3: Add Spices
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Cook the Bhindi
  1. Add the sautéed bhindi to the masala and mix gently.
  2. Add salt and dry mango powder. Mix well.
  3. Cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally to ensure the bhindi absorbs the flavors and does not stick to the pan.
Step 5: Finish and Serve
  1. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  2. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your delicious Bhindi Masala!
